hom

'Hom' is an conceptual space designed for public installation made in collaboration of Tharit Firm Tothong, Monthon Paoaron and Tharadol Panburana.

 

In the context of modern technology, people are distracted from physical world and direct interactions by the world of flat screens. Mobile Phone, tablet device, laptop, computer, television, etc. Static constructed world might not enough to satisfy us anymore.

 

Out of our common interest on how architecture could interact with, and at the same time, define human behavior. Hom consisted of an interactive ceiling made of fabric that attract people to comes together.

 

The project featured in Association of Siamese Architect 2010 and Venice Biennale Architecture 2010 as part of Thailandia Pavilion.
