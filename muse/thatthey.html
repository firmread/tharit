that they

'That they' is a local multiplayer iPad game made in collaboration of Tharit Firm Tothong and Peng Paul Cheng for MFA in Design and Technology in Parsons The New School For Designs.

 

iPad has been treated as personal device since its popularity, and applications developed on iPad are mainly designed for a single user. As device with such a huge screen and supporting of multi-touch, iPad  not only could be treated as a personal device. You can share iPad with your friends and family members. Moreover, we are in an information society and isolated by screens. We hope that the strange  condition this game provided could change it. In addition, we can find the feeling of talking and playing with players face to face. On the other hand, iPad adopts a lot of new technologies, which yet to be explored. For example, iPad possesses accelerometer sensor, which is frequently used for adjusting interface orientation. This game is an attempt to explore possibilities on user interface and gameplay in this parallel gesture.

 

The game entirely developed in OpenFrameworks for iOS.
