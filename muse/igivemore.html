iGiveMore

iGiveMore is a web startup for launching charitable crowdfunding and cause marketing campaigns that leverages social media (Facebook and Foursquare apps), smart data & game mechanics. Individuals get an interactive visualization of how they inspired others to donate. Organizations get data on who is raising the money & where the donation came from (e-mail, facebook etc). Businesses get a powerful tool to maximize the reach and brand value of their cause marketing campaigns.

 

iGiveMore UI/UX process is a collaborative process done with the CEO himself, Matt Bishop, our friend and my design mentor, Fabian Grateroles. While I am the one who implement everything from the wireframe to interactive prototypes and final design.
